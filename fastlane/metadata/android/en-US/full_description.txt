Rush Hour is a timetable app for Mumbai's suburban railway network. Look up train schedules between any two stations on the Western, Central, and Harbour lines.

Features:
- Search trains between any two stations
- Filter by fast/slow services
- Save favorite routes for quick access
- Share your live train position with other commuters
- Works offline with a bundled timetable database
- Configurable server URL for self-hosted backends
- Material You theming
