Common questions

  • Train times look wrong. Rush Hour uses official timetable data from Mumbai's suburban railways. If a scheduled time seems off, it may reflect a recent timetable change we haven't picked up yet. Let us know and we'll investigate.
  • Location sharing isn't working. Make sure you've granted location permissions in your device settings. Rush Hour needs "Always" or "While Using" access depending on whether you want background sharing.
  • The app shows no departures. Check your internet connection. If you're online and still seeing nothing, the backend may be temporarily unavailable. Try again in a few minutes.

Report a bug

When reporting a bug, it helps to include your device model, OS version, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are welcome.
